Lunar Punk

Final master project

About

Lunarpunk is the first Tower Attack ever made. A brand-new experience based on moving forward with your turrets to destroy the cosmic menace. You have a mission to accomplish, destroy the outer world enemies with the help of your turrets. The enemies will come out from mysterious portals, and your main objective is to close them with your own magic. But this won't be easy, because the enemies will keep coming and coming until you reach the portals, increasing the difficulty of the game as soon as you keep moving forward. The game was developed by Walking Octopus Games in Unreal 4 as part of Master 's Degree in Video Game Development of the Universidad Complutense de Madrid, by a group of more than thirty people in different departments and supervised by professionals of the industry.
