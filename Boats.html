About

Developed in unreal in just three days, "Barcos en plaza mayor" is a game in which you will have a diverse selection of ships based on legends of Madrid in which you will have to face other ships in the middle of Plaza Mayor. You will have four types of ships at your disposal, each with unique abilities, and you will have to face these same ships while dodging explosive barrels scattered around the map, be the last one standing and claim victory.

What did i do?

In this project I was involved as a programmer, and among the tasks I carried out were:

  • Boat movement
  • Ghost ship that when passing through other ships causes damage to them
  • Explosive and healing barrels
  • Life component
  • Ship animations
  • Custom AI of the ghost ship that will try to pass through the player's ship and other
  • Minor tasks such as bug fixing
